How To

Planting dahlia tubers:

If you've never planted a dahlia, follow a few simple steps to get them started:

Select a tuber with a visible eye or sprout, dig a well in the soil, place tuber in center and cover in about 1 inch of dirt, then pat soil gently to set the dirt around the well and you're all set to go!
