Intro to Gardening

Come learn the basics of how to start and keep your own gardening.

Planting-Dahlias"

We will teach you how to start successfully growing dahlias in your garden.

Weeding-and-Fertilizers"

Come learn all you will need to know about our local weeds and types of fertilizers that will benefit your garden and situation.

Dahlia-Varieties

We will discuss the appearance of different types of dahlias and what healthy specimens should look like.

Dahlia-Care"

We will teach you how to best care for your dahlias throughout the growing season.

Tuber-Preservaion-Techniques"

Come learn how to take care of the tubers in the off-season so you can propagate each plant and have healthy blooms for many years.
